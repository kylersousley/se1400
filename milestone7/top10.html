As a coaster enthusiast, one of the things we love doing is ranking our favorite rollercoasters we've been on. So far I have ridden 50 different rollercoasters (or as the enthusiasts would say, I have 50 credits), from across 9 different parks (and counting!). So here is my top 10!

Top 10 Ranking:

  • 1. Velocicoaster (Universal's Islands of Adventure)
  • 2. Twisted Colossus (Six Flags Magic Mountain)
  • 3. Wonder Woman Flight of Courage (Six Flags Magic Mountain)
  • 4. Iron Rattler (Six Flags Fiesta Texas)
  • 5. Wonder Woman Golden Lasso Coaster (Six Flags Magic Mountain)
  • 6. Tatsu (Six Flags Magic Mountain)
  • 7. Full Throttle (Six Flags Magic Mountain)
  • 8. West Coast Racers (Six Flags Magic Mountain)
  • 9. X2 (Six Flags Magic Mountain)
  • 10. Cannibal (Lagoon)
